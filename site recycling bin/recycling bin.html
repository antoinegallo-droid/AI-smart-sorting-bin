Waste is a Global Problem

Every day millions of tons of waste are produced around the world. Many people want to recycle, but sorting waste correctly is difficult. Mistakes in recycling reduce efficiency and increase pollution.

Our solution is the AI Smart Sorting Bin, a new technology that helps people sort waste automatically.

Our Product

The AI Smart Sorting Bin is an intelligent trash bin that uses artificial intelligence to recognize and sort waste.

  • Automatic waste recognition
  • Touchless and hygienic
  • Modern and durable design
  • Real-time waste monitoring

How the Smart Bin Works

The bin uses a camera and an artificial intelligence system to scan the waste. The system recognizes the material such as plastic, paper, or organic waste.

After the analysis, the bin automatically sends the waste to the correct compartment. This makes recycling easier and more efficient.

Who Can Use This Technology

The AI Smart Sorting Bin is designed for places with a lot of waste production.

  • Cities and public spaces
  • Schools and universities
  • Companies and offices
  • Airports and train stations
  • Shopping centers
